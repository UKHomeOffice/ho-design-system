Mandatory training for all new starters

The following training is available for new starters.

User-centred design induction This session is about user-centred design (UCD) at the Home Office, our best practice standards and what resources are available to help you meet those standards. This induction is aimed primarily at those starting in DDaT as user researchers, service designers, content designers and interaction designers. UCD Induction Trello This is the UCD Induction Trello which contains the essential things you need to know as a designer or researcher at the Home Office Introduction to accessibility This short training will cover: What is accessibility? Why do we need to be accessible?How do we go about that? Introduction to user research ethics at the Home Office (module 1) This is module 1 of the 4-part ethics training. Everyone must do module 1 but only user researchers should register for modules 2, 3 and 4. Ethics training for user researchers only Data handling for user researchers (module 2) Ethical principles for user researchers (module 3) Researcher and participant safety (module 4) Training for content designers only Content review ('2i') training All new content designers should do 2i training so they can check other content designers' work before they publish.
